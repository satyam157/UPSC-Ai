# 🤖 Ask Esu - Personalized Study Planning & AI Analysis

## Overview

**Ask Esu** is your personal AI study guide that generates comprehensive, personalized study plans based on your quiz performance, goals, and target exam date. It analyzes all your quiz data to provide actionable insights, workflows, practice strategies, and exam-cracking techniques.

- Customized to your learning style and goals

### 2. **Time-Bound Planning**

- Revision timelines aligned with your exam

### 3. **Workflow & Daily Routine**

## How to Use Ask Esu

### Step 1: Access Ask Esu
- Navigate to the sidebar
- Click on **"Ask Esu"** option

### Step 2: Provide Your Input

#### Input Your Query/Prompt
Describe your study goals or challenges:
- **Example:** "I'm weak in Current Affairs and want a plan to improve before UPSC Prelims"
- **Example:** "I'm struggling with Mains answer writing, need a specific strategy"
- **Example:** "Help me create a comprehensive revision plan for all UPSC subjects"

#### (Optional) Select Your Exam Date
- Click on the date picker
- Select your target exam date
- You'll see the countdown of days remaining
- This helps Esu create a time-optimized study plan

### Step 3: Generate Your Study Plan
- Click the **"🚀 Generate Personalized Study Plan"** button
- Esu will analyze your performance and generate insights

Contains:

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific in Your Prompt**
   - Instead of "help me study", try "I struggle with retention of Current Affairs"
   - The more details, the better Esu can personalize

2. **Set Realistic Exam Dates**
   - Provide a date that gives you enough prep time
   - Esu will factor this into your study load

3. **Take More Quizzes First**
   - Ask Esu works better with more quiz data
   - Try to have at least 5-10 quizzes completed

4. **Check Performance Metrics**
   - Always review the metrics tab for your actual statistics
   - This helps you track progress

5. **Follow the Plan Iteratively**
   - Generate a new plan every 2-4 weeks
   - Adjust based on your actual progress
   - Re-run with updated prompt if needed

## Understanding Your Metrics

### Accuracy
- **70%+**: Good - You're on track
- **50-69%**: Average - Needs improvement
- **<50%**: Weak - Needs immediate attention

### Marks
- Calculated as: `(Correct×2) - (Wrong×0.66)`
- Mimics real exam scoring system
- Higher marks = better performance

### Strong vs Weak Areas
- Determined by average accuracy in each subject
- Guides your study priority

### Accuracy Trend
- Shows improvement/decline over time
- Helps identify if strategies are working

## Troubleshooting

### "No quiz data available"
- **Issue:** Start by taking some quizzes (CA Quiz, PDF Quiz, Practice)
- **Solution:** Complete at least 3-5 quizzes before using Ask Esu

### "Study plan is too generic"
- **Issue:** Your prompt wasn't specific enough
- **Solution:** Provide more details about your struggles or goals

### "I don't see my recent quizzes in metrics"
- **Issue:** Metrics may not include latest data
- **Solution:** Refresh the page or regenerate the plan

### "Export file is empty or corrupted"
- **Issue:** AI response may be incomplete
- **Solution:** Try generating again, or check internet connection
